Manufacturing Process

Zinc Oxide Production Flow

High Quality Raw Materials

We use high-grade zinc metal and carefully selected raw materials to ensure purity and consistency in every batch of Zinc Oxide.

  • Premium Zinc Ingot Selection
  • Strict Quality Inspection
Melting Process

Zinc is melted in high-temperature furnaces under controlled conditions to prepare it for the oxidation stage.

  • Advanced Furnace Technology
  • Temperature Controlled System
Oxidation Process

The molten zinc reacts with air to form high-purity Zinc Oxide through a controlled oxidation process.

  • High Purity Output
  • Uniform Particle Formation
Cooling & Collection

The Zinc Oxide particles are cooled and collected using advanced filtration systems to maintain quality and fineness.

  • Dust-Free Collection
  • Fine Particle Control
Packaging & Dispatch

Final product is packed in high-quality bags with proper labeling ensuring safe transport and long shelf life.

  • 25kg / 50kg HDPE Bags
  • Custom Branding Available
Applications

Zinc Oxide Industry Uses

High purity Zinc Oxide used across multiple industries for superior performance and quality.

Rubber Industry

Improves strength, durability and heat resistance in rubber products.

Ceramic & Tiles

Enhances glaze quality, brightness and surface finishing in ceramics.

Paint & Coatings

Provides UV protection, anti-corrosion and long-lasting finish.

Pharmaceutical

Used in ointments, creams and medicinal formulations for skin.

Cosmetics

Essential ingredient in sunscreens, powders and beauty products.

Agriculture

Used as micronutrient fertilizer to improve crop yield and soil health.
